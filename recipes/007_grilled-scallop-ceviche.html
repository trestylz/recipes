Grilled Scallop Ceviche

Servings: 4 TO 6

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ pounds sea scallops (preferably dry-packed)
  • 2 tablespoons minced seeded jalapeño pepper
  • 1 cup Mojito Marinade
  • 2 tablespoons finely diced red bell pepper
  • 1 lime, cut into 8 wedges

Instructions

  1. Fire up the grill; then get back inside and fish the scallops out of the marinade. Dry them off really well, and string them onto skewers ( see note ). Pour the marinade into a small saucepan and bring it to a rapid boil. Toss in the red peppers, turn the heat to low, and simmer for 2 minutes. Keep warm.
  2. Grill the scallops over the hot coals for about 2 minutes on each side, or til the edges are tinged with a rich brown color and the flesh turns opaque.
  3. Ring a plate with lime wedges. Slide the scallops off the skewers and pile them up in the center of the plate. Pour on the sauce and serve family style, with a bunch of toothpicks so everyone can dig in.
