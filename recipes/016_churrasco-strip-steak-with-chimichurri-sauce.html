Churrasco Strip Steak with Chimichurri Sauce

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • The Steak:
  • 4 strip steaks (10 to 12 ounces each)
  • 1 cup Mojito Marinade
  • The Sauce:
  • 1 medium bunch fresh Italian parsley
  • 10 to 12 large cloves garlic, coarsely chopped
  • 3 to 4 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juice (about 1 lemon)
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 cup extra virgin olive oil

Instructions

  1. Whip up a batch of sauce. Pick off the parsley leaves to make a packed cup. Toss them in a processor or blender with the garlic, lemon juice, red pepper flakes, salt, cumin, and oregano. Process to a paste, scraping down the workbowl several times. With the processor running, slowly dribble in the olive oil til fully blended. Taste and fix up the seasonings. Set aside.
  2. Fire up the grill. Take the steaks out of the marinade and pat dry. Grill over hot coals til medium-rare (see Touch or Temp ). When they’re done, pull them off the grill and throw them on some plates. While they’re still sizzling, spoon some sauce over each steak and let the flavors and aromas blow your mind.
