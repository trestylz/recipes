Vaca Frita Pan-Fried Shredded Beef Patties

Servings: 6 TO 8

Ingredients

  • The Patties:
  • Ropa Vieja , made without the Cuban Creole Sauce
  • 1 cup Mojito Marinade
  • Olive oil for frying
  • The Accoutrement:
  • Black Beans & Rice

Instructions

  1. Grab one handful of shredded meat at a time, squeeze out as much of the marinade as you can, and shape the meat into a flattened patty about the size of your palm. Cover the bottom of a large skillet with ⅛ to ¼ inch of oil, and get it heated up over medium. Add the patties and fry a few at a time in the oil, cooking them til crispy and richly browned on each side. Watch them carefully so they don’t burn.
  2. Slide the fried patties onto a plate and serve ’em up with Black Beans & Rice.
