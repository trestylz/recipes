Cajun Corn

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup butter
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 3 cups fresh or frozen corn kernels, cooked
  • 5 teaspoons Creole Seasoning
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh Italian parsley

Instructions

  1. Toss the butter in a skillet and melt it over medium-high heat. Stir in the garlic and cook just til soft but not brown. Dump in the corn and give it a stir. Add the Creole
  2. Seasoning and cook for several minutes to warm up the corn and give it a bit of a toasty taste. Stir in the parsley. Scrape everything into a bowl and take it to the table.
