Peanut Butter Pie

Servings: 6 TO 8

Ingredients

  • The Crust:
  • 20 Oreo sandwich cookies
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted
  • The Filling:
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla
  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened (preferably Philadelphia)
  • 1 ¼ cups smooth peanut butter
  • 1 cup confectioners’ sugar
  • The Garnish:
  • ½ cup chopped peanuts

Instructions

  1. Put the cream, vanilla, cream cheese, peanut butter, and confectioners’ sugar in a mixer bowl. Mix on low for 1 minute, til blended, then gradually increase the speed to high. Whip til light and fluffy, about 1 more minute.
  2. Spoon the filling into the chilled pie shell. Even off the top and sprinkle with peanuts. Pop into the fridge and let it chill til set, about 4 hours. Serve chilled and cut into wedges. Don’t leave it out at room temperature for too long because it will soften too much.
