Creole Seasoning

Category: Dinosaur Pantry

Ingredients

  • ½ cup paprika
  • ½ cup granulated garlic
  • ¼ cup granulated onion
  • 3 tablespoons black pepper
  • 2 teaspoons white pepper
  • 2 teaspoons cayenne pepper
  • ¼ cup dried oregano
  • ¼ cup dried thyme
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 2 tablespoons sugar

Instructions

  1. Combine everything in a bowl and mix it up real well. Store in a plastic or glass container with a lid.
