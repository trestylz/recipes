Chicken Chop Suey

Tags: asian, chicken, stir fry

Ingredients

  • 4 cloves of garlic minced
  • 4 slices of ginger (2 minced)
  • 4 stalks of green onion sliced and separated
  • 6 oz celery sliced diagonally
  • 7 oz beansprouts
  • 6 oz chicken sliced
  • —— marinade ——
  • 2 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp oyster sauce
  • 4 tsp water
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 5 tsp cornstarch
  • 1/8 tsp white pepper powder
  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • —— cooking ——
  • 1/16 tsp sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp rice wine
  • 1 tsp rice wine
  • 2 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp dark soy sauce
  • 1/4 tsp sugar
  • 1/8 tsp black pepper powder
  • —— corn starch mixture ——
  • 1 tsp corn starch
  • 1/2 cup water

Directions

  1. 1
  2. Mince four cloves of garlic and prepare four slices of ginger take two of the slices and finely mince them. 2
  3. Next prepare four green onions spread the stem part in half lengthwise then cut it into two inch sections keep the stem part and the leafy part separate because we will use them at different times during cooking 3
  4. Cut celery diagonally into strips 4
  5. Cut bean sprouts if needed 5
  6. Cut the chicken into slices and marinate it with soy sauce, oyster sauce, some water a little bit of baking soda, some corn starch, and white pepper powder mix it up evenly and then mixing a touch of sesame oil 6
  7. Prepare the corn starch and water mixture 7
  8. Add some oil to a hot pan over medium high heat when the oil is hot add a little bit of salt and sugar to the oil and immediately add the celery stir fry the celery for about 20 seconds and add a touch of rice wine give the celery a few more tosses so that the rice wine evaporates and we can remove it from the pan 8
  9. Add a little more oil to the pan and add the two slices of ginger to the oil let the ginger sizzle in the oil briefly then add a little bit of salt and sugar to the oil and immediately add the bean sprouts to the pan, stir fry the bean sprout for about 15 seconds then add a touch of rice wine, give the bean sprout a few more tosses to let the wine evaporate and we can remove it from the pan 9
  10. Add a little more oil to the pan when the oil gets hot we can add the chicken spread the chicken out and let the pan fry briefly once the bottom is somewhat golden turn it over to pan fry the other side after briefly pan frying both sides add the minced garlic, minced ginger and the stem part of the green onion to the pan now stir fry everything together briefly and we can season the chicken with a little more soy sauce and a touch of sugar. 10 10. Continue to stir fry the chicken until the color looks even then we can pour the corn starch and water mixture into the pan mix the chicken and the cornstarch and water mixture together the liquid in the pan will quickly boil and thicken up into a sauce 11 11. Once the sauce has thickened we can add the bean sprouts and celery back to the pan also add the leafy part of the green onion and some crushed black pepper now all we have to do is give it few quick toss to mix everything together and we are done
