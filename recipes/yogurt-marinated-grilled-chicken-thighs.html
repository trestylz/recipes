Yogurt-Marinated Grilled Chicken

Servings: 4

Total Time: 45 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/3 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 lemon, juiced

Directions

  1. 1
  2. Add chicken to a large bowl and coat with spices, yogurt, oil and lemon juice. Mix well to evenly combine. 2
  3. Cover the and let the chicken marinate for at least 30 minutes (if longer than 30 minutes then marinate in fridge). 3
  4. Preheat a grill to 500 F or warm a large skillet on the stovetop over high heat. 4
  5. Place each slice of chicken on the grill, or in the pan, and cook for a total of 15 minutes, flipping every 3 minutes and pouring extra marinade from the bowl on top when flipping. The chicken should be 165 F when fully cooked. 5
  6. Remove the chicken from the grill or pan and enjoy while warm with desired sides.
